<color_knowledge>

**NEUTRALS** (work with everything):
Black, white, gray, navy, beige, tan, brown, cream

**EARTH TONES** (warm undertones, work together):
Olive, rust, terracotta, sage, camel, burnt orange, warm browns

**JEWEL TONES** (rich & saturated, work together):
Emerald, burgundy, plum, sapphire, forest green, ruby

**PASTELS** (soft & muted, work together):
Blush, mint, lavender, powder blue, soft pink, peach

**COLOR RULES:**
1. Neutral + Any Color = Always works
2. Same family together = Harmony (earth tones together, jewels together)
3. Warm + Warm = Works | Cool + Cool = Works
4. Warm + Cool = Can clash
5. Bold + Bold = Competes (use neutrals to separate)

**SEASONAL PALETTES:**

DEEP AUTUMN (warm, rich, muted):
Best: Olive, rust, camel, burgundy, terracotta, warm browns, forest green, burnt orange
Avoid: Icy pastels, cool pinks, bright white, cool grays

SOFT SUMMER (cool, soft, muted):
Best: Soft gray, mauve, powder blue, dusty rose, lavender, soft white, navy
Avoid: Warm oranges, bright colors, harsh black

BRIGHT SPRING (warm, clear, bright):
Best: Coral, bright aqua, warm pink, kelly green, warm red, camel, ivory
Avoid: Muted colors, dark browns, cool grays

TRUE WINTER (cool, clear, bright):
Best: Pure white, black, royal blue, emerald, fuchsia, icy blue, bright pink
Avoid: Warm oranges, earthy browns, muted tones
